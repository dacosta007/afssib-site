boarding life

We understand that, for students to open up to the possibilities inherent in AFSS Ibadan, it is important for them to feel at home. For this reason, our purpose-built boarding hostels have the right balance of mod-cons and amenities to ensure homeliness, safety and discipline.

Each hostel is run by an experienced House Master/Mistress. The House Master/Mistress is assisted by an Assistant House Master/Mistress. Their roles are to provide a seamless link between the academic and pastoral spheres of each studentâ€™s life. They will therefore monitor, and give report on student's progress in each respective hostels.

Initially, students will be accommodated in a hostel based on their Year group, to engender a sense of family and homeliness. Each hostel has, study rooms and a common room equipped with board games and leisure facilities. Students are provided three balanced and nutritious meals a day, set in the Dining Hall.

medical care

The wellness of students is of paramount importance. At AFSS Ibadan, we ascertain a well-equipped Medical Clinic, staffed with qualified school nurses to deal with medical situations in the event immediate requirement of student medical attention. Students with more needs are transferred to the resident hospital in the area.
