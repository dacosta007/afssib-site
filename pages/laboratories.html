laboratories

where we carry out

evidence based tests

At AFSS Ibadan, we put in place standardized laboratories for carrying out pratical experiments by students and teachers alike. Either be it prove of concepts or test based studies in all required subjects or courses within the school. There are different types of laboratories within the school: to properly serve required needs of varrious experiments which could be carried out in required subjects. Below are laboratories within AFSS Ibadan:

chemistry laboratory

This laboratory help serve students or teachers alike in carrying out experiments effectively and efficiently either from chemical based test, to analysis of biological specimen samples, to basic science experiments. Hence, this laboratory provides the conviniencies in carrying out both emperical, and forensic experiments quite easily.

physics laboratory

The physics laboratory within the school provides enough facilities to carry out evidence based tests encompassing physical natural phenomenons. Either be it prove of concepts concerning electricity, motion light spectrums, waves or sound, the laboratory is sufficient to handle these requirements. More so, the laboratory also helps to carry out experiments in introductory technology and much more.

ICT laboratory

The Information Communication Technology (ICT) laboratory helps provide computer literacy education to the students and as well carry out research, experiment on, and how computer or ICT works. The emerging technology of this contemporary age hovers around ICT, hence; this laboratory help gives an experimental and usecase of of ICT, and how it could be applied in our day-to-day endeavours.
