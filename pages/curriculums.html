by which we educate

syllabus

Apostolic Faith Secondary School, Ibadan. We employ both the Nigeria national curriculums which prepare our students for either the "Junior Secondary Certificate Examination" (JSCE), "National Examination Council Junior School Certificate Examination" (NECO), "West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination" (WAEC or WASSCE). And the international standard curriculums to also prepare our students for exams as IGCSE, SAT, BECE, and other international examinations.

Hence; this gives a framework for impacting enough knowledge and skills into our students to form, and have a well-rounded education scope. All this help keeps our students well-informed and well equiped for present and ,future time.

Our classrooms size are kept relatively small to a ratio of 1:20 (teacher/students) delibrately, which helps improve interactions between teachers and, students. Which has proven to be more effective, and efficient communication in classrooms. More so, the delivery of our rich curriculums are made through our teaching specialist staffs who are capable of getting the job done.

Classroom work is complemented by projects, course work, assignments, practicals, and expeditions to places of interest, so as to have a profound, and practical knowledge that is reliable.

A seperation of

concern by schools

At AFSS Ibadan, we have schools seperated into two classification to serve required purposes more effective and efficient in a robust fashion to give proper adequate knowledge required to students in accordance to their age gaps. Therefore, below are the classifications of our school;

JSS (Junior Secondary School)

This school classification phase is typically for students between the age 11 to 14. A wide range of subjects is mapped out to develop their knowledge, understanding, and talents through our broad, and balanced curriculum. Therefore, a careful tracking of students attainments duely monitored by the school and such reports sent over to student's parent or guardian.

More so, because of our careful and meticulous preparations(ref. to curricular syllabus), all students are a worthy cadidate to seat for both Nigeria exams(NECO, WAEC), and international assessments(i.e IGCSE, BECE)

SSS (Senior Secondary School)

Under this school classification, which is typically for students between the age of 15years and above; A wider range of subjects/courses are introduced for proper and advance knowledge-base to deeping much futher our student's knowledge, and understanding.

This classification school phase is the final stage for preparing our students into universities. A proper counselling is offered to each of our students in making a decisive decisions on courses/deciplines to pursue in the university by how well they do in their attainment report in school.

Although, their 3years span in the school(under SSS classification) which is the total years expected to be spent. All of the students are worthy enough to seat for the Nigeria exams at the senior level (i.e; JAMB/UTME, WAEC, NECO), and other international assessments test (i.e; SAT, IGCSE, TOEFL etc.).
