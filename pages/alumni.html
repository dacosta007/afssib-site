Alumni community

the Apostolic Faith Secondary School Alumni Association(AFSSAA) community, Ibadan Chapter, is the coming together of past, and present old students which have graduated, and have ever attended the school at some period in time. AFSSAA community help her alma mater work hand-in-hand, to keep the school moving forward to higher level of excellence. And thus, foster better relationship with other sister branches within the AFSS school paradigm.
