admission

welcome to admission page

We are glad to have you on our admission page. Here we help you make certain, securing an admission place for your child or ward in aseemless way possible. In all perspectives, this page help provide information on our admission procedures/processes that each parent/guardian may have grounded understanding on how admission into our school(s) works. Please note, before a child or ward is or can be admitted into the school, they have to pass through these following stages:

assessment stage
At this level, the school is required to conduct an entrance examination test, to unequivocally make certain the child's abilities and performances. Which help provide a good indication of a child's current attainment level, but more importantly, reveals his or her overall abilities and potentials.

interview stage
This is the second stage, here, each potential students will be query by the school's interview team. Which they(potential students) will be graded. This is to make sense of verbal dialogue with them which helps the school the get a first-hand interaction experience with the type of potential student we are dealing with.

provisional admission letter
Here is the final stage to those who score average and above marks (50% or greater of 100marks) on the above two stages(assessment & interview). If the child is short-listed as part of the successful potential students, he or she is qualified to get admitted into the school. As a confirmation SMS, and Email will be sent to the parent's contact media provided, for further action to be taken in completing the admission of their child or ward into the school. Please note, the provisional admission letter is to be printed in the child's account on the admission portal and then brought along on child's resumption into the school.

a step-by-step guide

for form purchase

1
getting to portal

If all the requirements for completing the form is satisfied you can then head over to the school's admission portal for further processes in completing the form.

2
account creation

To start any process on the admission portal, you need to create a new account for your child or ward (as a new user else, you could login to proceed). with and email address (we recommend opening a new email for your child which can't take up to 1minutes A Gmail email address is more preferable). So that you may proceed to filling the admission form.

3
fill form details

After all required information is filled about you child or ward on the form, you will be directed to where payment for your form is to be made. You then be directed to the next step of the form purchase.

4
save and make print-out

This is the last step required in the purchase of the school's admission form, where, when all successful steps has being completed, you will be presented with an entrance examination slip which can be save, and printed ( on Desktop: ctrl + p. With all the above steps successfully done, you will be redirected to your dashboard on the admission portal where you can track and receive status on your child's performances and admission state (either admitted or not yet admitted).
