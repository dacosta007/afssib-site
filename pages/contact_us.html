FAQ

some frequently ask questions

You may be curious about some things which are necessary to be inquisitive about, and imperative towards any decisions that is to be taken. Below are some of the fundermental, and familiar answers to question that have, and may be asked. And should you consider these information insufficient, feel free to contact us through any of the above media channels, we will be glad to respond to any query in no time, thank you.

What do you fees covers?

It encompasses lunch, educational materials, and a one-time registration fee.

is lunch provided?

yes, we provide healthy, nutritious, cooked meal for our students.

do you run a bus system

not at the moment. But will be making such available soon.

how do I buy and apply for an admission form

we have make it easier for parent to purchase our admission form for their child or ward on the the school's admission portal. Follow this link to the admission portal portal to secure one for your child.

Entrance exams preperation and requirement

subjects to be taken for exams are English, and Mathematics, also an interview will be conducted to affirm the child. But before all this is to be conducted, the child must be with the examination print-out slip, which is printed when the admission form was purchase. Hence, serves as a prove of application/payment.

How can I know my child's entrance exam scores?

you can get your child's examination scores and admission status on the school's admission portal you created when you bought the admission form.

when given admission, what should I do

As it will be a provisonal admission, a counter offer should be follow up by the parent, in making all necessary steps need to complete the admission process. This can be done through the child's account on the admission portal. So there is no need be having uncertainty, we make the process easier to safe guard such admission.

how can i purchase the school wears & uniforms

you can head over the school's website, on the uniform page to make purchase and place wears & uniforms order on required wears of your choice.
